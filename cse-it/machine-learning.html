Overview of the program

Machine learning is the study of computer algorithms that can improve automatically through experience and using data. It is a branch of artificial intelligence (AI) and computer science. Machine learning is changing the world, it has a presence in many major industries including healthcare services, education, transport, food, entertainment and more. It will impact almost every aspect of our daily lives, housing, cars, shopping, food ordering, etc. will all become easier and more efficient with the help of machine learning.

01Why is machine learning becoming so important?

In the past Decade, the growth and implementation of Artificial Intelligence has been immense, one of the major contributors to this has been the study of algorithms, or machine learning. It is an entirely data driven concept that analyses user behavior and business operational patterns regardless of the field it is applied in.

02Is a career in machine learning rewarding?

A career in machine learning can be highly rewarding for those interested in developing and applying algorithms and statistical models to analyze and make predictions from large amounts of data. This growing field offers many opportunities for career growth and advancement, as the demand for machine learning experts continues to increase. Additionally, many companies are investing heavily in this field, leading to competitive salaries, benefits, and job security for those with relevant skills and experience.

03Why Skill Lab?

At Skill Lab we place an emphasis on equipping our interns with the practical skills necessary to succeed. This doesn’t mean however, that the subject’s theory is exempted from our course. We will teach you the necessary theoretical concepts and practical skills to succeed. We will also let you get hands-on experience from our top associated mentors who are distinguished in their respective fields.
