Overview of the program

The increasing need to find solutions to the degradation of our environment has led to the increasing prevalence of electric vehicles on Indian roads. Indeed, finding a mode of transportation that is eco-friendly is the need of the hour. By adopting electric vehicles on a large scale, we cut down on carbon emissions from vehicles that are damaging our atmosphere. It will also give us an alternative source of fuel.

01Why are electric vehicles becoming more popular?

Electric automobiles are the vehicles for the future, they are also gradually becoming the vehicles of the present. Electric scooters are becoming very popular in India. They provide the same if not better performance than their petrol counterpart while also being eco-friendly and more economical in the long run.

02Is a career in electric vehicles worth pursuing?

Is car design rewarding?

Many Automotive giants have their design and development centers in India. Car design is both a lucrative and competitive field in the market. Freshers can expect to make a starting salary between 2-3 lacs per annum. The average salary of senior car designers is around 8 lacs per year.

03Why Acmegrade?

At Acmegrade we place an emphasis on equipping our interns with the practical skills necessary to succeed. This doesn’t mean however, that the subject’s theory is exempted from our course. We will teach you the necessary theoretical concepts and practical skills to succeed. We will also let you get hands-on experience from our top associated mentors who are distinguished in their respective fields.

01Why are electric vehicles more expensive?

Although the price of electric vehicles is higher than petrol alternatives, electric vehicles are not in fact more expensive than petrol vehicles. The money you spend on fuel will be saved in it’s entirety. In the long haul an electric vehicle will prove to be the more cost-efficient purchase.

02How long is the training?

Our self-paced and mentor led courses both last for a period of 2 months. Our advanced courses last for a period of 3 months.

03Will Acmegrade help with placements?

Acmegrade provides placement support for all students of our advanced courses. Students that perform well during their internship period in our self-paced and mentor led courses will also get placement support.

04What types of batteries do electric vehicles use?

While there are several forms of batteries used in electric vehicles, the most common is lithium-ion batteries. These are the same kind of batteries used in phones and laptops but are designed with the needs of an automobile in mind.
